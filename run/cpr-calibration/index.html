Chemical P removal calibration

Haas et al, 2001

With known PO4e, PO4i and Me_P_mole_ratio measures, find a1 and a2 such that:

PO4e = a1 · PO4i · e-a2·(Me_P_mole_ratio)

The above equation can be linearized (y=mx+n) as:

ln(PO4e/PO4i) = ln(a1) - a2·(Me_P_mole_ratio)
where:
  • y = ln(PO4e/PO4i)
  • x = Me_P_mole_ratio
  • m = - a2 (slope)
  • n = ln(a1) (intercept)
